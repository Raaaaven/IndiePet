Your first Task

Go talk to a classmate you don’t usually hang out with. Start a convo, maybe about homework, a funny meme, or anything random. The goal? Make a new friend. When you’ve actually done it, hit Complete, we’re trusting you here.

Your Second Task

Join a new online voice chat group. Don’t just sit muted, talk for at least 10–20 minutes, even if people ignore you at first. Keep going, because the win here is proving you can hold your ground. Hit Complete only when you’ve survived the full time.

Your third Task

While you’re out walking, give a genuine compliment to a random stranger. Could be about their outfit, their shoes, or even their vibe. The goal is to spark a quick positive interaction—easy, short, done. Complete it once you’ve made someone smile.

Your forth Task

Head to a bookshop and ask a random customer to recommend you a book. Don’t just take the title and leave, actually have a short convo about it. Ask why they liked it, what type of reader it’s for. When you’ve had a real back-and-forth, mark Complete.

Your fifth Task

Go to a café or shop alone and order something. But here’s the thing: when your order arrives, say “Oh, I forgot to mention, no onions please” (or politely tell them if they got it wrong). Don’t just accept it and walk away, wait until they fix it and only leave once you’ve got the correct order in your hands. Hit Complete when you’ve stood your ground.
